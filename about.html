About Barangay 172 Urduja

Barangay 172 Urduja is a vibrant residential community in Caloocan City. We deliver responsive frontline services, promote safety and public health, support youth and livelihood programs, and practice transparent, tech‑enabled governance to uplift every Urdujeño household.

Barangay 172 Official Seal

The official seal of Barangay 172 Urduja symbolizes service, unity, and progress. It represents our commitment to transparent governance and community-driven development for every resident of Urduja Village.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide efficient, transparent, and accessible public services that enhance the quality of life for all residents while promoting community participation and development.

Vision

To be a model barangay that exemplifies good governance, digital innovation, and community empowerment in the 21st century.
